Cognizant Technology Solutions

Graduate Analyst July 2017 - December 2019

I joined Cognizant's graduate programme in 2017 and I sit in the Artificial Intelligence and Analytics unit. I underwent 4 months of training to get equipped with essential knowledge in database, ETL, data visualization and technical skills in SQL, Tableau, Qliksense and Python.
After the training programme, I joined a bank's global data warehouse team. I extracted and analyzed large volumes of client data using Informatica ETL and Oracle database. I performed data integrity checks using SQL to convey findings to various stakeholders.
